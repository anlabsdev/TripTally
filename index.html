✦ Workflow

Get Started in 3 Simple Steps

From download to your first logged expense in under a minute.

01

Create Your Trip

Add a trip with a name, destination, date range, budget, and your preferred currency. TripTally keeps each journey perfectly organized.

02

Log Expenses Instantly

Tap a category tile or the + button to open the Add Expense sheet. Enter amount, pick a category, add a note, and choose the payment method.

03

Get Smart Insights

Head to the Summary tab for visual breakdowns — total spent, daily average, monthly and yearly summaries with beautiful charts.
